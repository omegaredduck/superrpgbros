## Bugs found → FIXED (all verified in-engine)

**1. Knight auto-attack whiffed on targets you could see the blade hitting**
*(your report: "every other hit isn't doing damage" — confirmed, root-caused)*
`scenes.js meleeSwing` tested only the mob's CENTER POINT against range/arc.
Hi-fi mobs draw 40px wide, so a brute visually overlapping the slash whiffed
whenever its center sat just past the 94px range or the 115° arc edge — the
eye said hit, the math said miss. The cleave is now **target-size-aware**:
each mob adds its own radius to the reach and widens the angular window by the
angle it subtends. If the blade visibly clips a body, it connects. Verified:
range-edge and arc-edge overlap targets now take damage; far targets still miss.

**2. Level-ups handed the Knight a free full RAGE bar** *(high)*
`entities.js grantXp` refilled `mp` to max on every level-up — for the Knight
that meant a free ~full whirlwind (with lifesteal) every level, breaking the
"rage is earned by the cleave, never given" design. A `startsEmpty` resource
now keeps its current value through a level-up (HP still fully heals).
Verified: rage 20 stays exactly 20 across a forced level-up.

**3. Pausing silently ate every new timed effect — including the train telegraph** *(high)*
The scene clock keeps running while paused, and `unfreeze()` only shifted shot
lifetimes. So: the train horn's 1300ms warning could fully elapse behind the
ESC menu and the **instakill train launched the same frame the menu closed**
(zero telegraph); tornadoes vanished on resume; frost slows expired; splitter
bolts forked instantly; boss pattern cooldowns fired immediately. `unfreeze()`
now shifts ALL absolute clocks: train phase timers, tornado lifetimes +
re-hit clocks, mob slowUntil, projectile splitAt, boss radial/stream timers.
Verified: +1.5s pause shifts tornado/slow/train clocks by exactly the paused time.

**4. The train killed players frozen by hitstop** *(high)*
Hitstop pauses physics (player can't move) for 40ms bursts — up to ~30% of the
time under sustained fire — but the train moves manually and kept advancing
(~42px per burst) with no i-frames on contact. The train now freezes during
hitstop, same as everything else.

**5. Trains kept ambushing the death screen** *(medium)*
No alive/closing gate: after "YOU DIED", warnings, horns, rumble SFX, and
camera shake continued forever under the overlay. New trains no longer start
once the fight is decided; a pending warning cancels; a train already rolling
finishes its pass and the yard goes quiet. Verified both gates.

**6. A spawned portal leaked ACROSS SAVE SLOTS** *(medium)*
`pendingPortal` lives in the game-wide registry and was only consumed on
entry. Spawn a portal in slot 1, exit to the load screen, load slot 2 → slot
2's chamber materialized slot 1's fully configured portal. The title screen
(the slot boundary) now clears it. Verified.

**7. A corrupt save could brick the title screen for ALL slots** *(medium — crash)*
`SAVE.migrate()` dereferenced nested fields before `valid()` ran, outside any
try/catch: a JSON-valid but gutted save (`{"v":3}`) threw an uncaught
TypeError inside the title's slot cards. Any throw during migrate/validate now
reads as "corrupt" (the slot shows as corrupt; everything else works). Verified.

**8. Builder playtests silently loaded the train yard instead of your map** *(medium)*
The hi-fi hardwire bypassed the mapId path entirely, so the builder's
PLAYTEST ▶ button tested… the gravel yard. An explicit `mapId` (only the
builder passes one) now takes the real map path; normal portal runs still get
the train yard. Verified: mapId run loads the map, hifiWorld off for that run.

**9. Missing art module = field of missing-texture squares instead of the classic fallback** *(low — resilience)*
`hifiWorldOn()` returned true unconditionally; if `world_art.js` ever failed
to load, the realm still routed to the train yard (and mobs got hi-fi
scales/bodies on 16px art) with none of the textures built. It now flips on
only after the hi-fi world textures actually build — mirroring how the
chamber already gated on `_chamberBuilt`. Verified true after normal boot.

**10. Resize/fullscreen during the portal-entry fade destroyed the run config** *(low — edge case)*
`enterPortal` consumed `pendingPortal` immediately, then started the Realm on
a 450ms delay; a resize in that window restarts the Nexus, cancelling the
start with the portal already consumed — no portal, no run, config lost. The
portal is now consumed at the moment the Realm actually starts; a cancelled
entry leaves it standing to walk into again.

## Not bugs — design notes worth knowing

- **Hi-fi player hurtbox is ~2x the classic (40x48 vs 20x24 world).** The
  models are 2x on-screen by your pick, and the body uses the same 0.625/0.75
  fractions as classic — hits match what you see. Flagging it as a BALANCE
  knob: if dodging ever feels unfair at 64px, shrink `d.body` in the model
  descriptors. (The misleading "hitbox is unchanged" comment was corrected.)
- **Train-killed mobs award no XP/kill credit** — same silent-wipe pattern as
  the E2 swarm wipe; wave-director bookkeeping stays correct. Intentional.
- **The train passes through the boss** (hits only player + mobs). Reads fine
  in play; a boss-clipping train would be a balance grenade anyway.
- The staff's star tip clips ~1px at the texture edge (invisible in-game);
  wizard/knight card previews and all pooled-sprite resets checked clean.
- Everything else probed came back clean: pooled-sprite resets, splitter
  fork re-entrancy, overlap callback arg order, champion/frost tint
  interplay, chamber ambient/powerUp tween lifecycles across resize restarts,
  save schema migration ladder, music track beat sums, menu layout after the
  ART TEST section removal.
