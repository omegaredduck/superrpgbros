# THE GRAND ENGINEER — 2-Phase Boss Design (biome 4)

*Proposed kit for Red's approval/reshape. Phase-1 look = engineer sheet #5 "The
Overseer". Phase-2 = mech sheet #10, renamed **PROTOTYPE 130C-4**. Fought in a
custom arena off the regular map. Precedents: Grovekeeper phase-two, Gravekeeper
5-wave immunity.*

---

## The arena — "THE PROTOTYPE BAY"

A **square room** (not the procedural map). Tiles: riveted steel floor, hazard
strips at the edges, two **in-ground conveyor belts** crossing the middle (the map
mechanic follows you into the fight). At the **top (north) wall** sits the dormant
**PROTOTYPE 130C-4** on a raised **boarding gantry with a ladder** — visible, powered
down, optic dark, the whole fight long. It is the phase-2 threat you can see coming.

Environment features the boss weaponizes (this is the "environment-based kit" you
asked for):

- **Structural PILLARS** — 4 heavy support columns spaced around the room. They're
  **cover**: they block line-of-sight for the Reactor Overload (see below) and stop
  bullets. The whole arena is built around them.
- **Ceiling hydraulic presses** — 4 of them over marked floor pads.
- **Wall-mounted rivet turrets** — 4, one per wall, dormant until deployed.
- **The two conveyor belts** — he can surge/reverse them to shove you.
- **Coolant vents + electrified floor panels** along the edges.

**Design principle (yours):** the kit is fully **factory-themed**, and the boss AI is
**environment-driven** — he doesn't fight in a vacuum, he *operates the room*. Every
decision keys off the machinery: which press to drop, which belt to surge, which
turret to wake, when to overload the reactor. The room is his weapon and his tell.

---

## PHASE 1 — THE GRAND ENGINEER (on foot)

Squishier, mobile, ranged; he never melees — he makes the **room** attack you while
he keeps distance and tinkers. Entrance: **rises through the floor on a hydraulic
lift**, steam venting. Roughly a Grovekeeper-sized HP pool.

1. **RIVET VOLLEY** *(filler)* — fires a short 3-round aimed burst from his
   tablet-slaved drone. Dodgeable, constant pressure.
2. **TURRET DEPLOY** *(environment)* — slams his tablet; 2 wall turrets power on and
   track-fire at you for ~8s. Ignoring them stacks damage; they power back down after.
3. **PRESS SLAM** *(environment, CIRCLE readout)* — a **circular ground telegraph**
   (like the graveyard cone-mob readout you liked) lights under a ceiling press; ~1s
   later it **stamps** for heavy damage. Fires 2-3 presses in sequence — read the
   circles, keep moving.
4. **CONVEYOR SURGE** *(environment / mechanic)* — overclocks or reverses a belt to
   drag you toward a hazard while another attack lands. Punishes standing on belts.
5. **ARC DISCHARGE** *(CIRCLE)* — smacks a generator; an **expanding ring of electric
   arcs** blooms outward from him (circular telegraph → you run outward/inward to the
   safe band).
6. **CALL THE LINE** *(adds)* — summons 2 factory mobs from your roster (Sparkbots or
   a Hive Drone) as harassers, capped low.
7. **COOLANT FLOOD** *(environment, slow field)* — vents freeze 2 floor patches into
   slow-zones for a while; shrinks your safe footing.

**End of phase 1 → the cutscene.**

---

## TRANSITION CUTSCENE (your spec)

Engineer goes **invulnerable**, sprints to the **ladder** at the top of the room,
**climbs up and drops into PROTOTYPE 130C-4's open cockpit**. The mech's visor flares
on, servos boot, exhaust stacks fire — then it **knocks the boarding platform/gantry
clean out of the way** (it topples aside with a crash) so nothing stands between it
and you. It steps down off the dock. **Phase 2 begins.**

---

## PHASE 2 — PROTOTYPE 130C-4 (the mech)

Bigger, tankier, aggressive — it **comes after you** (slow relentless pursuit) instead
of holding back. Drill arm + gatling arm, open cockpit (the Engineer visible, red eyes
inside). Fresh HP bar, enrages at low HP.

1. **GATLING SWEEP** *(spread)* — the gatling spins up and rakes a fan of bullets across
   your last position. Strafe out of the arc.
2. **DRILL CHARGE** *(line, telegraph)* — winds up aiming at you (~1s), then **dashes
   across the room** drilling; leaves a torn-floor gash. Sidestep the lane.
3. **GATLING CIRCLE** *(CIRCLE / bullet-ring)* — plants and fires a **radial ring** of
   bullets outward — a circular pattern with gaps to slip through.
4. **MORTAR BARRAGE** *(CIRCLE readouts)* — lobs 4-5 shells that paint **circular
   telegraphs** on the floor, then detonate. Keep relocating.
5. **STOMP SHOCKWAVE** *(CIRCLE)* — stomps; an expanding **shockwave ring** radiates —
   be outside the band when it hits.
6. **ASSEMBLY OVERRIDE** *(environment)* — hijacks the arena: **all conveyors to max +
   presses hammer on a rhythm** while it pressures you. The room becomes the attack.
7. **REACTOR OVERLOAD** *(signature — the self-destruct / cover mechanic)* — the mech
   stomps to the **center of the room**, plants, and starts **BEEPING and FLASHING**
   red, faster and faster, warning klaxons blaring (long, unmistakable telegraph).
   Then it **detonates a room-wide blast** — anything with line-of-sight to the mech
   is hit for massive (near-lethal) damage. You **survive by ducking behind a PILLAR**
   to break line-of-sight before it blows. This is *not* a true death — it's an
   overload it vents and recovers from, then keeps fighting (an enraged tell it does
   ~once per phase, maybe twice when low). Standing in the open = you're gone.
8. **OVERCLOCK (enrage, low HP)** — vents blow, molten exhaust trails, gatling fire
   rate and move speed ramp up for a frantic finish; Reactor Overloads come sooner.

Kill the mech → **boss down** → reward chest. (Optionally, a beat where the cockpit
pops and the Engineer slumps — a clean "you win.")
